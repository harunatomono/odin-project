Lasagna Recipe

Description

Okonomiyaki is a vegetable pancake originating from the Kansai region of Japan and is particularly popular in Osaka (大阪). It can be a healthy and nutritious meal, and easily made to accommodate dietary restrictions.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • baking powder
  • eggs
  • tenkasu
  • green cabbage
  • sliced pork belly, shrimp, or other type of protein

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl to make a chunky batter.
  2. Oil pan well.
  3. Cook on one side until nicely colored. Then flip for additional color.
